About Our Company


First Industry Company (FIC) is a provider of comprehensive fire protection and industrial safety products, headquartered in Jaro, Iloilo City, Philippines. The company is dedicated to preventing the loss of lives and property due to fire incidents by offering a wide range of fire safety solutions.

FIC's flagship product is "Fire 101," which includes various types of high-quality, non-toxic, and environmentally friendly fire extinguishers. The company specializes not only in the manufacture of fire extinguishers but also in the installation, design, and inspection of fire detection and alarm systems.

FIC operates under a commitment to safety, reliability, and quality assurance, ensuring that their products and services meet stringent industry standards. They target multiple sectors, including residential, commercial, industrial, healthcare, and educational institutions, helping clients achieve compliance with fire safety regulations.

Overall, FIC aims to create a safer environment by investing in innovative technology, providing expert services, and actively engaging in community fire safety education.
