About Our Company


PGA Green Global Solutions Co. is a company based in the Philippines, specifically in Iloilo City, that operates in the general construction industry and serves as a distributor of various merchandise. The company specializes in providing comprehensive solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects with a strong focus on quality and sustainability. They are known for their specialized waterproofing services, which stand out as their flagship offering.

PGA Green Global Solutions Co. is the authorized applicator of Pioneer Pro Products in the Western Visayas region, ensuring the use of high-quality materials in their projects. The company also offers a range of other services, including epoxy floor coating, general construction, installation of fire detection systems, and more, aimed at meeting the diverse needs of their clients. They are committed to excellence, innovation, and environmentally friendly practices, positioning themselves as a trusted partner across various industries.

Overall, PGA Green Global Solutions Co. aims to provide effective construction solutions while prioritizing customer satisfaction and environmental responsibility.
